The Story of Ichumma's Inn

Nestled in the lush heart of Wayanad, Ichumma’s Inn began its journey in 2017 as a dream project by the award-winning Humming Tree Architects. What started as a weekend home soon transformed into a warm and welcoming homestay – a space where comfort, privacy, and nature come together beautifully.

Unlike traditional stays, Ichumma’s Inn offers you something truly rare – complete privacy. As a single-house property, the entire space is reserved for just one group at a time. No shared walls, no unfamiliar faces – just you, your loved ones, and the peace of Wayanad’s green hills.

Our homestay is equipped with everything to make your stay memorable – a serene swimming pool, a full-sized basketball court, a cozy barbeque setup, and nights warmed by the glow of a campfire. Whether you’re looking to unwind or play, there’s something for everyone here.

And of course, no stay in Kerala is complete without its flavors. Our attentive staff are just a call away, ready to serve up delicious Kerala cuisine and offer help whenever you need it – while still giving you the privacy you value.
