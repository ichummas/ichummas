Rules and Policies

Please take a moment to read through the guidelines to ensure a comfortable and memorable stay.

1. General Rules

  • Only full-house bookings are allowed. No individual room bookings are available.
  • Maximum occupancy is limited to the number confirmed during booking.
  • Government-issued photo ID is required for all adult guests.

2. Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM onwards
  • Check-out time: 11:00 AM
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon prior request.

3. House Rules

  • Please respect the peaceful surroundings and avoid loud noise after 10:00 PM.
  • Smoking is not permitted inside the house.
  • Pets are not allowed, unless explicitly approved prior to booking.

4. Swimming Pool Guidelines

  • Pool usage is allowed between 7:00 AM and 9:00 PM.
  • Children must be supervised by adults at all times.
  • No diving or running near the pool area.

5. Barbeque & Campfire Policies

  • Campfire and barbeque can be arranged upon request.
  • Guests are responsible for maintaining safety around fire sources.
  • Please inform the staff if you need assistance with lighting or setup.

6. Visitors

  • Only registered guests are allowed to stay overnight.
  • Daytime visitors must be approved by the host in advance.

7. Damages & Liability

  • Guests are liable for any damage caused to the property or items therein.
  • Please report any accidental damage immediately to the management.

8. Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations made 7 days prior to check-in are fully refundable.
  • No refund for cancellations within 7 days of the stay or for no-shows.

9. Privacy Policy

  • Guest information will be used solely for booking and stay-related communication.
  • We do not share guest data with any third parties.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our policies, feel free to contact us.
